SERVICE

National Residence Hall Honorary

National Residence Hall Honorary(NRHH) is a group for on-campus residents at A&M. One of the goals of NRHH is to provide service. In the past we've done projects to help the local animal center and veterans. We also work to recognize exceptional students and programs on campus.

Big Event

Big Event is a huge service event that happens every year. Groups of Aggies are sent to residents in Bryan/College Station area to do various service projects. I’ve done Big Event every year I’ve been at A&M and usually get a group together when the time comes. In the past, projects usually include activities like landscaping, picking up trash, and painting.
